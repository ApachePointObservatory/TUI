TUI:Instruments:Shack-Hartmann

The Shack-Hartmann window provides complete control over the Shack-Hartmann wavefront sensor.

Much of the window is the standard Expose Window; only Shack-Hartmann-specific items are described in this document (or will be once Shack-Hartmann-specific controls are available).

Note: unlike many other instruments, the Shack-Hartmann wavefront sensor has no separate configuration window.
